Bringing The Outside In

Introduction

Bringing plant life into your home is shown to have several benefits to not only your mental wellbeing, but also physical health many studies have shown the abilities of household plants to filter the air of not only carbon dioxide, but also other potentially harmful toxins that fill the air in your home. Along with improvements to physical health, plants have been reliably demonstrated to have numerous psychological benefits, with factors such as mood, concentration, stress, and productivity all being influenced by the presence of plants in the surroundings.
